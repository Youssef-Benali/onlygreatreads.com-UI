Only Great Reads Philosophy

In the early stage when Only Great Reads was founded, the idea was to provide the registred subscribers with only great reads as book suggestions.Since we placed the bar of quality on how we define a great read at the highest point we could think of, and after an extensive research by our team, we couldn't find as many great reads as we thought we would. Then, we included the other kind of book suggestion 'Books Worth Reading' besides 'Great Reads'. Ultimately, our philosophy is to be able at some point in time to provide our subscribers only with 'Great Reads' as books recommendations. By becoming a registred subscriber, you give life to bringing to fruition the vision of Only Great Reads to the world.
